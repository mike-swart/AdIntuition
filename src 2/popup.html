Welcome to AdIntuition

This Browser Extension will help you determine when there is a relationship between a YouTube content creator and advertiser. These relationships are determined through the presence of Affiliate Marketing Links, which trace the source of traffic to a web page and, if products are purchased, the YouTube content creator that brought you there earns a commission. Though affiliated marketing is not always bad, it is important to realize when a video is sponsored in order to form a full opinion about the content of the video.

Known affiliate links are highlighted in red

Suspected affiliate links are highlighted in orange

Suspected coupon codes are highlighted in yellow

When you load a Youtube video, this extension will alert you in your preferred format (configured through your settings) if the video contains sponsored content.
