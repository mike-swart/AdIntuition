Welcome to AdIntuition

This Browswer Extension will help you determine when there is a relationship between a YouTube content creator and advertiser. These relationships are determined through the presence of Affiliate Marketing Links, which trace the source of traffic to a web page and, if products are purchased, the YouTube content creator that brought you there earns a commission. Though affiliated marketing is not always bad, it is important to realize when a video is sponsored in order to form a full opinion about the content of the video.

When you load a Youtube video, this extension will alert you in your preferred format (configured through your settings) if the video contains sponsored content.
